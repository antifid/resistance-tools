How to Use This Tool

This calculator helps you front-load donations into the early crisis years while keeping your long-term plan financially fair using present value. You do not need to understand the math â€” the tool handles it.

  1. Enter the Original Planned Amount: Your normal yearly donation.
  2. Enter the Post-Crisis Planned Amount: The smaller yearly donation you would accept later to give more now.
  3. Click Calculate. The calculator will tell you how much you should donate in the crisis years.
  4. (Optional) Open Advanced Options to change assumptions or customize year-by-year.
