Front-Loading Donation Calculator

Purpose of This Tool

This calculator helps you determine the appropriate front-loaded donations for the early crisis years while keeping your long-term plan financially fair using present value. You do not need to understand the math — the tool handles it.

For a reminder about front-loading, you can review pages 18-21 in the presentation slides.

Privacy

This calculator does not store or transmit any of the information you enter. Everything is done locally in your browser.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Enter the Normal Annual Donation: Your normal yearly donation.
  2. Enter the Percent to Shift to Crisis Years: The percent of your normal donation that should be moved into the crisis years.
  3. Click Calculate. The calculator will tell you how much you should donate in the crisis years and show the calculated post-crisis amounts.
  4. (Optional) Open Advanced Options to change assumptions or customize year-by-year.
